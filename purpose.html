Youth HIV Awareness & Prevention

The two-fold objectives of this theme are to provide young readers with accessible and stigma-free facts about HIV tests, and to emphasize the importance of knowing one's HIV status in a general prevention campaign.

Some youth are not exposed regularly to focused health communication work, especially in an environment where HIV is a taboo. This campaign helps normalize testing and connect students with health resources.

Why HIV Awareness Matters

Youth Impact

Young people account for a significant portion of new HIV infections globally each year.

Early Testing

Early detection through testing can significantly improve treatment outcomes and prevent transmission.

Break the Stigma

Open conversations about HIV reduce stigma and encourage more people to get tested.

Resources & Support

We provide confidential resources, testing information, and educational materials to help you make informed decisions about your health.
